Source of Dataset

Variables definition

Following are the Definitions and Data Types of various fields used in the dataset

Attribute Data Type Definition
Id INT Primary Key, Uniquely identifies the entries in table
First Name TEXT The given or first name of Noble Laureate
Surname TEXT Family name of the Noble Laureate
Born DATE Date of Birth of the Noble Laureate
Birth Location TEXT Country and City of Birth of the Noble Laureate
Nobel Received Year DATE Year when the Laureate received the Nobel Prize
Motivation TEXT Contribution for which Laureate received Nobel
Organization name TEXT Organization of the Nobel Laureate during the year when Noble was awarded
Organization's Location TEXT Country and City of the Organization of the Nobel Laureate when Nobel was awarded
